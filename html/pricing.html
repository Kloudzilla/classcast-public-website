Answering your questions

  • How long are the contracts?
    You can choose the billing period when signing up (1mo, 3mo or 12mo). You can start and stop your service any time.
  • What if I go over my chosen plan?
    Classcast limits your usage and users by the plans and notifies the account holder when the limit is approaching. We don’t automatically change or upgrade your plan.
  • Do you offer a discount for annual plans?
    Yes of course. We’re just in the process of making this automated, for now please let your sales associate know! Simple as that.
  • What about Music Licensing?
    Classcast offers a comprehensive music licensing plan that covers all your bases. Contact us to find out more.
  • What credit cards do you accept?
    We accept all major credit cards (VISA, MasterCard, Discover, American Express). For annual payments we can also invoice you for payment.
  • Need more help before signing up?
    If you have questions about Classcast, our pricing, features or the sign up process, please feel free to contact us at hello@classcast.co – we’re always here to help you!
  • Can I change plans?
    Yes all non-enterprise plans can be changed any time. Enterprise plans are managed by case-by-case basis.
  • What kind of security do you have
    Classcast uses secure 256-bit SSL encryption for all accounts and all data is stored securely. For more on privacy, see our privacy policies and terms of service.
  • Do you offer non-profit discounts
    While we don’t have specific discounts for non-profits or educational organizations, we do offer a discount when you sign up for an annual account. Please email us at hello@classcast.co as we might be able to help.
  • Hosting and storage included?
    All videos, music, text... well anything is hosted for as long as you remain with us.
  • What happens when I want to cancel an account?
    This would be a very sad moment, but we understand that these things happen. From your point of view, nothing. Once you cancel your account you’ll simply never be billed again. To cancel your account please just send as an email and we’ll process your request.
